📌 Why this analysis matters

Single-issue NGOs typically focus on their own covenant's core issues and miss intersectional populations:

  • CEDAW NGOs emphasize gender discrimination but may overlook the unique needs of disabled women.
  • CRC NGOs focus on children but find LGBTQI+ children sensitive and disabled children siloed across ministries.
  • CRPD NGOs center disability but rarely analyze the gendered/youth/indigenous dimensions.

Platform value: cross-referencing 3 government document corpora surfaces intersections every covenant misses, identifying entry points for joint cross-covenant advocacy.
