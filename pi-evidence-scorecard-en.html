📋 NGO advocacy prioritization tool
This scorecard gives 0-100 evidence strength scores to help NGOs decide:
  • A grade (70-100): Strong evidence, ready for media / hearings / parliamentary questions
  • B grade (50-69): Usable, but reinforce specific dimensions (e.g., yearly data)
  • C grade (30-49): Medium evidence, needs targeted supplementation
  • D grade (10-29): Thin evidence, needs foundational research / interviews
  • F grade (0-9): Very weak — requires FOI requests / fieldwork first
