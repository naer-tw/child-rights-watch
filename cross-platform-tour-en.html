📚 30-second introduction

A civic monitoring platform integrating 3 Taiwan civil society watchdogs — Women (CEDAW) / Children (CRC) / Disability (CRPD) — under a shared evidence_pipeline.py open-source backbone. Crawls 2,223 government documents, indexes 102,728 passages with FTS5, auto-links policy issues to evidence (1,266 linkages across 32 issues).

Unique value: cross-covenant comparison — surfacing intersectional gaps invisible to any single covenant NGO (disabled women, LGBTI children, indigenous women, etc.).
