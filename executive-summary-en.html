📋 Evidence quality (32 PI scorecard, 5-dim formula)

Each Policy Issue is scored 0–100 on 5 dimensions (passages 0–40, events 0–20, cosignatories 0–15, organizations 0–15, year span 0–10). Distribution:

  • 7 A-grade (≥70) — strong evidence, ready for media / hearings / parliamentary questioning
  • 20 B/C-grade (30–69) — moderate evidence, advocacy-ready with focused supplementation
  • 5 D-grade (<30) — government / society "fail to see" issues (4-dimension action plan in D-grade Research TODO)
