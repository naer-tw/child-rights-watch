問卷調查：關於大學入學考試分科測驗恢復國文 英文與數乙，你的看法為何？

在大學考招方面，問卷中有 72.8%的高比例認為應恢復分科測驗國文、英文、數

乙等考科。事實上，今年(2022 年)因為分科測驗不考數乙，許多商管科系分發時

只能參採學測數 A 或數 B 成績，甚至分科測驗的數甲成績，此種招生策略對於

社會組學生特別不公。又因為分科測驗不考國文、英文，學測命題老師在權衡之

下，增加了試題的難度。結果原本還能達到均標或後標的學生，直接摃龜。成績

不能達標，繁星、申請都上不了，連分發都不可能，已傳出多位高三學生已準備

明年重考。學測題目的難度增加，也進一步深化了課本與考題的鴻溝，降低學習

的效益，減少學習的熱情。如果未來七月的分科測驗仍然不考國英，恐導致學生

愛上不上國英的加深加廣課程，將令高三下國英兩科處於空轉狀況，弱化學生語

文素養，甚至弱化國力。

建議事項：

一、應大幅降低學習歷程檔案對於高中生學習與生活的衝擊：

從問卷統計結果可以看到，固然高中學習歷程檔案的製作過程或有助於學生個人

的成長，但恐未能充分呈現學生個人學習特色亮點，並將明顯因為城鄉/貧富差

距的不同而造成升學的不公，此外，多數高中生會刻意參加特定社團、營隊或活

動等以增加學習歷程檔案對教授的吸引力，從而使得高中的康樂性社團或與升學

無關的社團逐漸式微，影響高中生涯的發展，令人擔憂。我們認為，應大幅降低

學習歷程檔案對於高中生學習與生活的衝擊。

首先，學習歷程檔案系統應明確成為大學申請入學備審資料的輔助工具，不

要再規定每學期上傳的件數，但因儲存空間有限，可以訂定每一位高中生上傳資

料儲存空間的上限。此外，要增加高中學生及家長對於大學教授想要看到學習歷

程內容的認識，事實上，學習歷程是評估學生整體學習成果的佐證資料，如果只

有學習過程卻沒有實質的學習效果，那樣的學習歷程檔案是不具意義的，最重要

的還是學生自身素養的提升。

二、大力推動適性探索與輔導

許多高中生反映找不到學習歷程準備的方向，因此，要大力推動適性輔導，讓孩

子早一點進行適性探索，找到自己的興趣與性向。對於偏鄉及弱勢生，應提撥經

費協助其準備學習歷程檔案，避免因為資源的不足而造成不公平，甚而埋沒的人

才。

三、增加考試入學的名額

因「升學領導學習」是不可避免的狀況，學習歷程檔案的使用所以造成那麼多的

問題，就是因為大學申請入學的名額過高，考試入學的名額逐年降低，導致學生

不得不花很多的時間在準備學習歷程檔案，因此，我們建議要增加考試入學的名

額，甄選入學(非考試入學)及考試入學的名額應各占一半。

四、強烈建議應儘快恢復分科測驗的國文、英文、數乙等考科

因分科測驗不考國文、英文、數乙等考科，導致不少校系考試分發須回頭參採學

測成績，使得新課綱下的學測走上「一試定終身」的回頭錯路，造成學生參加學

測的壓力。因此，我們強烈建議應儘快恢復分科測驗的國文、英文、數乙等考科。

Suggestions:

1. Reduce the impact of the academic portfolio on the learning and

lives of high school students:

We can see from the survey that the process of making an academic

portfolio is beneficial to the personal growth of the student, but it

won’t show the student’s distinct learning characteristic and will

11

cause unfairness in the admission to college due to the urban-rural/

rich-poor gap. In addition, majority of high school students who want

to increase the attractiveness of their academic portfolio will participate

in a specific clubs, camps or other activities to impress the professors.

Thus, recreational clubs or clubs related to further studies on high

school campuses will become sparse, affecting the development of high

school careers, which needs to be worry about. We suggest to reduce

the impact of the academic portfolio on the learning and lives of high

school students:

First of all, the academic portfolio system should be used as an

auxiliary tool for the preparation in the admission of university

application. It is not necessary to stipulate the number of uploads per

semester. However, due to the limited storage space, the upper limit of

the storage space of each high school student's uploaded materials can

be set. In addition, it is necessary to increase the awareness of high

school students and parents about the content of the academic portfolio

required by the university professors. In fact, the academic portfolio is

a supporting material for evaluating the overall learning outcomes of

students. If the academic portfolio does not have substantial learning

12

effects, such academic portfolio is meaningless. What matters is the

improvement of students' self-literacy.

2. Promote adaptive counseling and adapative exploring

Many high school students reported that they could not find their

direction in preparation for the academic portfolio. Therefore, we

vigorously promote adaptive counseling, so that children can find their

own interests and aptitudes earlier through adapative exploring. For

remote and disadvantaged students, funds should be allocated to assist

them in preparing academic portfolio to avoid unfairness or even bury

their talents, due to insufficient resources.

3. Increase the quota of students in the entrance exams

Since "admission to higher education governs learning" is an

unavoidable situation, the use of academic portfolio has caused so

many problems due to the fact that the number of applicants for

admission to universities is too high, while the number of students

taking entrance exams decreases yearly, resulting in students having

to spend a lot of time in preparing their academic portfolio. Therefore,

we recommend to increase the quota of students through the entrance

13

exams process. The quota of screening-based admission(non-exam

admission) and that of the admission through entrance examination

should be equal.

4. Strongly recommend the resumption of the subjects - Chinese,

English, and Math B in the subject-test as soon as possible.

Since the subject-test does not contains the subjects of Chinese,

English, Mathematics B, many schools and departments have to refer

back to the academic test results for the distribution of examinations,

which makes the academic tests under the new syllabus embark on the

wrong path of "One test for life". This creates pressure for students in

taking academic exams. Therefore, we strongly recommend that

subjects such as Chinese, English, and Mathematics B in the subject-

test should be resumed as soon as possible.
