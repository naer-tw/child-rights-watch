🔬 Why this diagnosis

Wave 105 cross-covenant keyword comparison surfaced surprisingly low CRPD-platform coverage of CRPD's own core concepts:

  • Reasonable accommodation: CRPD 7 segments vs CEDAW 100 vs CRC 173 — the disability convention's flagship concept is least covered in disability documents.

  • Same-sex marriage: CRPD 0 segments — LGBTQI+ × disability intersection is untouched.
